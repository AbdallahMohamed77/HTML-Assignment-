Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.
He is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

About Him

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.He began his football career at a young age and quickly
rose to prominence with his exceptional skills and goal-scoring ability.
Ronaldo has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career, including multiple Ballon d 'Or titles.
He has played for top clubs such as Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus before joining Al Nassr in 2023.

Ronaldo is known for his incredible athleticism,
dedication to training, and philanthropic efforts off the field.
